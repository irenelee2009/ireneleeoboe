Biography

Irene Lee — Oboist

Irene Lee is a young oboist whose playing is defined by emotional depth, refined technique, and a natural musicality that reaches beyond her years. A student at The Juilliard School Pre-College Division, Irene began her oboe studies at a young age and quickly distinguished herself in competitions at both the national and international levels.

Irene has been selected for the National Youth Orchestra of the United States (NYO2) and the prestigious Verbier Festival Junior Orchestra in Switzerland, performing alongside some of the world's finest young musicians.

"The oboe speaks what words cannot — and Irene gives it a voice all her own."

In 2026, she was recognized by the National YoungArts Foundation with a Distinction award — one of the highest honors for young artists in America — and performed as a finalist in the International Young Artist Concerto Competition (IYACC). She won First Prize at the 2024 Juilliard Pre-College Open Competition and First Prize at the New Jersey Soloist Competition (Woodwind Division) in 2023.

Irene continues to develop her artistry with passion and dedication, bringing a rare combination of sensitivity and technical precision to every performance.
